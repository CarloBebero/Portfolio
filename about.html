ABOUT ME

Kumusta, I'm Carlo Bebero.

I am a second-year college student studying Interactive Media
Development at Confederation College in Thunder Bay, Ontario.
I enjoy expressing my creativity through graphic design, web
development and photography.


As a front-end web developer, I specialize in creating visually
appealing and user-friendly websites. I use various design
software and coding languages to bring my ideas to life, focusing
on aesthetics and user experience. My goal is to deliver impactful
designs that effectively communicate clients’ messages, exceeding
expectations and providing unique digital experiences.
